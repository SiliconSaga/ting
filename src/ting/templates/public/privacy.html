Privacy

  • No accounts, no emails, no passwords.
  • Your IP address is not stored — only ephemeral rate-limit counters keyed on a hash.
  • No third-party trackers, no cookies beyond a session token, no fingerprinting.
  • Analytics are page-view counts only via GoatCounter (no cookies, respects Do-Not-Track).
  • Your most-recent answer replaces prior answers; you can revise indefinitely.
  • Survey results are public but anonymous. Aggregated responses appear on the Survey results page for anyone to see; no entry can be traced back to a code, an envelope, or a person.
